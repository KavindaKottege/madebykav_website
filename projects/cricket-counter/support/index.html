Getting Started

üì≤ First Launch

  1. Open the Cricket Counter app on your iPhone
  2. Tap "Get Started" to begin setup
  3. Choose your match format (number of overs)
  4. Tap "Start Match" to begin scoring

‚åö Apple Watch Setup

  1. Ensure Cricket Counter is installed on your iPhone
  2. The Watch app will automatically appear on your Apple Watch
  3. Open the app on your Watch
  4. If you have an active game on iPhone, it will sync automatically
  5. Use the crown to navigate between different screens

üèè Basic Scoring

  • iPhone: Tap main area for legal deliveries, use dedicated buttons for extras
  • Apple Watch: Tap screen for legal deliveries, rotate crown to access extras
  • Wickets: Tap the "+Wicket" button
  • Wickets on Extras: Long-press "+Wicket" button for wide+wicket or no-ball+wicket
  • Undo: Use the undo button to reverse any action (24 levels available)

Troubleshooting

üîß Common Issues

App won't sync between devices

  • Ensure both devices are signed in to the same Apple ID
  • Check that Bluetooth is enabled on both devices
  • Make sure both devices are connected to WiFi or cellular
  • Try restarting both apps
  • On iPhone: Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements > Don't Share

Watch app not responding

  • Make sure the iPhone app is installed and working
  • Press and hold the side button, then slide to power off
  • Restart your Apple Watch
  • Try uninstalling and reinstalling the Watch app

Buttons not responding properly

  • Make sure you're tapping the button area, not just the text
  • For long-press gestures, hold for at least 0.5 seconds
  • Clean your screen and ensure it's dry
  • Restart the app if buttons remain unresponsive

Data lost after app crash

  • Your match data is automatically saved every action
  • Restart the app - your data should be restored
  • If using both iPhone and Watch, check the other device
  • Data is also backed up to iCloud automatically

üîÑ Reset Options

If you're experiencing persistent issues:

  1. Soft Reset: Close and reopen the app
  2. Hard Reset: Settings > Reset Innings > Start from 0.0
  3. App Reset: Delete and reinstall the app (you'll lose saved matches)
  4. Watch Reset: Remove and re-add the Watch app via the Watch app on iPhone
