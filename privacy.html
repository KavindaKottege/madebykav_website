Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how Cricket Counter protects your data.

Last Updated: January 2025

Overview

Cricket Counter ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Cricket Counter available on the Apple App Store for iOS and watchOS devices.

Key Points:

  • We collect minimal data necessary for app functionality
  • Your cricket match data stays on your devices
  • We use Apple's secure systems for data synchronization
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties
  • You have full control over your data

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Cricket Match Data: Overs, balls, wickets, deliveries, and match settings you input while scoring cricket matches
  • App Settings: Your preferences for overs count, reset options, and other customization settings

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: iOS/watchOS version, device model, app version for compatibility and troubleshooting
  • Usage Analytics: App launch frequency, feature usage patterns, and crash reports (only if you opt-in via iOS settings)
  • Session Data: App launch timestamps for determining whether to resume matches or start fresh

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number) unless voluntarily provided for support
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Contacts or photo library access
  • Social media profiles or external account information

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Uses

  • Core Functionality: Store and display your cricket match data, scores, and delivery history
  • Cross-Device Sync: Synchronize match data between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's iCloud and WatchConnectivity frameworks
  • Session Management: Determine whether to resume existing matches or start new ones based on app launch patterns
  • Data Persistence: Save your match progress locally on your devices and restore it when you reopen the app

2.2 App Improvement

  • Bug Fixes: Identify and resolve technical issues using crash reports and error logs
  • Feature Development: Understand which features are most valuable to users to guide future updates
  • Performance Optimization: Improve app speed, reliability, and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

  • Primary Storage: Your cricket match data is stored locally on your devices using secure iOS/watchOS data storage mechanisms
  • App Groups: We use Apple's App Groups feature (group.com.umpire.counter.app) to enable secure data sharing between the iPhone and Apple Watch versions of our app
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted using Apple's standard device encryption when your device is locked

3.2 iCloud Synchronization

  • Apple's Infrastructure: Cross-device sync utilizes Apple's secure iCloud infrastructure and WatchConnectivity framework
  • Your Control: Sync only occurs if you're signed in to iCloud and have iCloud enabled for our app
  • Encryption in Transit: All synced data is encrypted during transmission using Apple's security protocols

3.3 Security Measures

  • No external servers or databases - all data remains within Apple's ecosystem
  • Regular security updates through iOS/watchOS system updates
  • Secure coding practices following Apple's security guidelines
  • No third-party analytics or tracking services integrated

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do NOT Share Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your cricket scoring data remains private to you and is only accessible through your own devices signed in to your Apple ID.

4.2 Limited Disclosure Scenarios

We may disclose information only in these specific circumstances:

  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Safety Protection: To protect the safety, rights, or property of Cricket Counter, our users, or others
  • Business Transfer: In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, or acquisition (with 30 days notice)
  • With Your Consent: Any other disclosure will only occur with your explicit consent

4.3 Apple Integration

  • App Store: Basic app usage statistics may be shared with Apple as part of App Store analytics (you can opt-out in iOS Settings)
  • StoreKit: Subscription purchase data is processed by Apple according to their privacy policy
  • iCloud: Sync data is stored in your personal iCloud account, governed by Apple's privacy policy

5. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

5.1 Data Control

  • Access: All your data is accessible directly within the app - no need to request it from us
  • Modification: Edit or update your cricket scores and settings anytime within the app
  • Deletion: Delete individual matches, reset all data, or delete the app entirely to remove all data
  • Export: Your data remains on your devices and in your iCloud account for your use

5.2 Privacy Settings

  • iCloud Sync: Disable in iOS Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Cricket Counter
  • Analytics: Control app analytics in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements
  • Crash Reports: Disable automatic sharing in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements

5.3 Data Portability

Your cricket match data is stored in standard formats on your devices. If you choose to stop using Cricket Counter:

  • Your data remains in your iCloud account
  • You can continue to access it by reinstalling the app
  • Deleting the app and its data from iCloud permanently removes all information

6. Children's Privacy

Cricket Counter is designed for users of all ages who are interested in cricket scoring. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

  • App Store Rating: Cricket Counter is rated 4+ (suitable for all ages) on the App Store
  • No Personal Data Collection: The app doesn't require accounts, personal information, or social features
  • Parental Controls: Parents can control app downloads and in-app purchases through iOS Screen Time and restrictions
  • Educational Use: The app can be used by young cricket enthusiasts learning to score matches

If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information to us, you may delete the app and its associated data to remove such information promptly.

7. International Data Transfers

Cricket Counter is developed and operated from various locations. Your data may be processed in countries where we or Apple operate.

  • Apple's Infrastructure: Data sync utilizes Apple's global iCloud infrastructure, which maintains appropriate security standards worldwide
  • GDPR Compliance: For users in the European Union, we ensure compliance with General Data Protection Regulation requirements
  • Security Standards: All international data transfers maintain the same security and privacy standards described in this policy

8. Data Retention

8.1 Match Data Retention

  • Local Storage: Cricket match data is retained indefinitely on your devices until you delete it
  • iCloud Storage: Synced data is retained in your iCloud account according to Apple's retention policies
  • User Control: You can delete match data anytime using the app's reset features

8.2 Analytics and Support Data

  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated, non-personal usage data may be retained for up to 2 years for app improvement
  • Support Communications: Support emails and communications are retained for 3 years for quality assurance
  • Crash Reports: Technical crash data is retained for 1 year for bug fixing purposes

8.3 Account Deletion

Since Cricket Counter doesn't use accounts, there's no account deletion process. To remove all data:

  1. Delete the app from all your devices
  2. Remove Cricket Counter data from iCloud (Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage)
  3. Complete data removal occurs when app and iCloud data are both deleted

9. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons.

9.1 Notification of Changes

  • Material Changes: We will notify users of significant changes through app updates and notifications
  • Website Updates: The most current version will always be available on this website
  • Effective Date: Changes become effective immediately upon posting unless otherwise stated

9.2 Your Acceptance

By continuing to use Cricket Counter after privacy policy changes, you accept the updated terms. If you disagree with changes, please stop using the app and delete it from your devices.

10. Third-Party Services

10.1 Apple Services

Cricket Counter integrates with the following Apple services, each governed by Apple's Privacy Policy:

  • iCloud: For cross-device data synchronization
  • StoreKit: For in-app purchase processing (if applicable)
  • WatchConnectivity: For iPhone-Apple Watch communication
  • App Store: For app distribution and updates

10.2 No Other Third Parties

Cricket Counter does not integrate with any other third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media platforms. We believe in keeping your cricket scoring data private and secure.

11. Regional Privacy Rights

11.1 European Union (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right to Access: Request access to your personal data (available directly in the app)
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data (edit directly in the app)
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data (delete the app and iCloud data)
  • Right to Data Portability: Your data is stored locally and remains portable
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data

11.2 California (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to Know: This privacy policy describes what data we collect and how it's used
  • Right to Delete: Delete the app and its iCloud data to remove personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We won't discriminate against you for exercising privacy rights
  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell personal information to third parties

11.3 Other Jurisdictions

We respect privacy rights in all jurisdictions and will comply with applicable local privacy laws.

12. Privacy Policy Summary

Quick Reference:

  • ✅ Minimal Data Collection: We only collect data necessary for app functionality
  • ✅ Local Storage: Your cricket data stays on your devices and iCloud
  • ✅ No Third-Party Sharing: We don't sell or share your data with anyone
  • ✅ User Control: You have full control over your data at all times
  • ✅ Secure Transmission: All data sync uses Apple's encrypted infrastructure
  • ✅ No Tracking: No advertising trackers or external analytics
  • ✅ Transparent Practices: This policy explains exactly what we do with your data
  • ✅ Privacy by Design: Privacy protection is built into Cricket Counter's core design

Bottom Line: Cricket Counter is designed to keep your cricket scoring data private, secure, and under your complete control.
